Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026  •  Whizzpack Packing Solutions, Rajkot, India

Our Commitment

Whizzpack is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and individuals who contact us for product enquiries. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide voluntarily through our contact and enquiry form: your name, company name, country, business email address, phone number, product interest, and any additional details you choose to share. We do not collect any information automatically beyond standard web server access logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL), which are retained for security and diagnostic purposes only.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our enquiry form is used solely to respond to your request for a product quote, sample, or general information about our corrugated boxes and cotton seed bags. We do not sell, rent, license, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

If you contact us via WhatsApp or email directly, we use those messages only to respond to your enquiry.

Data Retention

Enquiry and contact data is retained for up to 24 months to support ongoing business relationships and for legitimate business record-keeping. After this period, data is securely deleted. Web server logs are retained for up to 90 days.

Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Enquiry data is transmitted via a third-party form submission service (Formsubmit.co) using SSL encryption. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your information from unauthorised access or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

Google Fonts — loads typography assets. Google may log the request, including your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Formsubmit.co — processes enquiry form submissions and forwards them to our email. No data is sold or shared by Formsubmit.

Unsplash — serves some blog images from their CDN. Unsplash may log requests.

We do not use advertising networks, social tracking pixels, or analytics cookies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us at info.whizzpack@yahoo.com. We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for these requests.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We do not use session cookies, advertising cookies, or persistent tracking technologies. Your browser may cache static assets (images, scripts) for performance, which is standard behaviour and does not involve personal data.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business-to-business enquiries. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
