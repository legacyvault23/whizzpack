Product Range

Six Box Types, One Factory

From lightweight retail cartons to industrial bulk containers, every style is manufactured in-house and shipped factory-direct.

01

Regular Slotted Carton (RSC)

The global standard for export shipping. Four uniform flaps meet at the centre, minimising waste while delivering consistent compression strength across FCL and LCL shipments. Available in every size and ply combination.

3-ply to 7-plyAny dimensionSingle and full-colour printKraft or white liner
02

Die-Cut Boxes

Precision-cut to exact dimensions using custom steel-rule dies. Fold patterns are engineered for your specific product, reducing void fill and improving shelf presentation. Perfect for retail-ready and branded export packaging.

Custom die shapesFull CMYK printAuto-lock bottomTuck-top options
03

Telescoping Boxes

Two-piece lid and base construction for fragile, high-value exports. The deep overlapping lid provides tamper evidence and superior stacking strength compared to standard tuck-top cartons.

Lid and base setsCustom depthHigh stacking BCTPremium finish options
04

Double Wall Corrugated

Two fluted medium layers bonded between three liners for significantly enhanced compression and puncture resistance. The go-to choice for heavier goods on long-haul sea freight to USA and UK.

5-ply constructionHeavy-duty gradeHigh BCT ratingElectronics and machinery
05

Triple Wall Corrugated

Three fluted layers for absolute maximum strength. Handles industrial machinery, automotive components, bulk agricultural commodities, and any product where box failure is not an option.

7-ply constructionIndustrial gradeMax load capacityReplaces wooden crates

Custom Printed Boxes

Full brand packaging with your logo, colour palette, barcodes, handling pictograms, and regulatory text. Up to 6-colour flexo printing on any corrugated grade. Minimum order applies per design.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions US and UK importers ask before buying corrugated boxes from India.

What is the minimum order quantity for corrugated boxes?

Our standard MOQ is 5,000 units. Custom sizes, board grades, and printed boxes are all available from this quantity. Contact our export team to discuss trial order options for a first order.

Do you export corrugated boxes to the USA and UK?

Yes. We export directly from our factory in Rajkot, India to importers in the USA, UK, Europe, Canada, and Australia. All shipments include full export documentation: commercial invoice, packing list, HS code declaration, and certificate of origin.

What types of corrugated boxes do you manufacture?

We manufacture Regular Slotted Cartons (RSC), die-cut boxes, telescoping boxes, double-wall corrugated, and triple-wall corrugated boxes. We also produce custom-printed boxes in full-colour CMYK or single-colour for branding and retail shelf use.

Are Whizzpack corrugated boxes ISO certified?

Yes. Our manufacturing facility holds ISO 9001:2015 certification. We use BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) compliant raw materials and can supply quality certificates and test reports with every shipment.

What is the lead time for corrugated box orders?

Standard production lead time is 15-20 working days after artwork and specification approval. Sea freight transit to the USA takes approximately 25-30 days and to the UK approximately 22-28 days. Express production is available for time-sensitive orders.

What flute types and board grades do you offer?

We produce corrugated boxes in B-flute, C-flute, E-flute, and BC double-wall configurations. Board grades range from 3-ply to 7-ply, with burst factor ratings from 14 BF to 30 BF to suit your load and stacking requirements.
