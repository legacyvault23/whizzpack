Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions US and UK buyers ask most when sourcing packaging from India.

Does Whizzpack export corrugated boxes and cotton seed bags to the USA and UK?

Yes. We export to the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and across Europe. Our factory in Rajkot, Gujarat ships via sea freight to your port of choice with full export documentation including HS codes, certificate of origin, and ISF filing support.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 5,000 units for corrugated boxes and 5,000 bags for cotton seed bags. Custom sizes, printing, and branding are available from MOQ. Contact us to discuss your volume requirements.

How long does shipping take from India to the USA or UK?

Production lead time is 15-20 working days. Sea freight to the US West Coast (Los Angeles or Long Beach) takes approximately 25-30 days. To UK ports (Felixstowe or Southampton) it is approximately 22-28 days. Air freight is available for sample shipments.

Is Whizzpack ISO certified?

Yes. Whizzpack operates under ISO 9001:2015 quality management certification. Our products meet international export quality standards required for markets in the USA, UK, and Europe. Quality certificates are available with every shipment on request.

Can I get custom printed boxes and bags with my brand?

Absolutely. We offer full-colour CMYK printing, single-colour screen printing, and unbranded options for both corrugated boxes and cotton seed bags. Share your artwork and we will prepare a free pre-production proof before production begins.

What quality checks are done before shipping?

Every order goes through a multi-stage QC process: incoming raw material inspection, in-process production checks, and a final pre-shipment inspection. We also support third-party pre-shipment inspections by agencies such as SGS or Bureau Veritas at your request.

Can I request a sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes. We offer complimentary sample packs for buyers in the USA and UK. Contact us with your product specifications to request your free sample.
